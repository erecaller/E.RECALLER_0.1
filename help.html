How to Use

How do I learn about different tenses?

Go to the Lessons section from the main navigation. Select any tense category (Present, Past, Future) to see all available tenses. Click on a tense card to view detailed explanations, structure, and examples.

How can I practice what I've learned?

Navigate to the Practice section. Select a tense you want to practice from the tabs. Answer the multiple-choice questions and get immediate feedback with explanations.

How does the scoring system work?

Each correct answer increases your score. Your progress is shown as a percentage, and you'll see your skill level (Beginner to Expert) based on your performance.

Can I switch between languages?

Yes! In the Lessons section, each tense explanation has tabs for English, Sinhala, and Tamil. Click on your preferred language tab to view content in that language.
