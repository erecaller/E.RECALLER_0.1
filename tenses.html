Present Tenses

Simple Present Tense

Simple Present Tense is used for things that happen every day, things that are always true, or actions that happen regularly.

Simple Present Tense කියන්නේ දිනපතා, නිතරම, හෝ සාමාන්‍යයෙන් සිදුවන ක්‍රියා විස්තර කරන්න යොදාගන්න ක්‍රියාවක්.

Simple Present Tense என்பது தினசரி நடக்கும், பழக்கம், அல்லது உண்மை விஷயங்களை சொல்வதற்காக பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • Singular Subject + Verb (Base form) + s/es + Object
  • Plural Subject + Verb (Base form) + Object

Examples

  • Singular: She goes to school.
  • Singular: He buys groceries.
  • Plural: They go to school.
  • Plural: You like to cook.

Present Continuous Tense

Present Continuous Tense is used for actions happening now or temporary situations.

Present Continuous Tense කියන්නේ දැනට සිදුවෙමින් පවතින ක්‍රියා හෝ තාවකාලික තත්වයන් විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Present Continuous Tense என்பது இப்போது நடக்கும் செயல்கள் அல்லது தற்காலிக சூழ்நிலைகளுக்கு பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • Singular Subject + is + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object
  • Plural Subject + are + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object
  • Subject I + am + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object

Examples

  • Singular: She is studying right now.
  • Plural: They are playing football.
  • I: I am reading a book.

Present Perfect Tense

Present Perfect Tense is used for completed actions with present relevance or experiences.

Present Perfect Tense කියන්නේ අවසන් වූ ක්‍රියා වර්තමානය සමඟ සම්බන්ධතාවයක් ඇති විට හෝ අත්දැකීම් විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Present Perfect Tense என்பது முடிந்த செயல்கள் ஆனால் தற்போதைய முக்கியத்துவம் கொண்டவை அல்லது அனுபவங்களை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • Singular Subject + has + Verb (Past participle) + Object
  • Plural Subject + have + Verb (Past participle) + Object

Examples

  • Singular: She has finished her work.
  • Plural: They have visited London.
  • Plural: We have seen that movie.

Present Perfect Continuous Tense

Present Perfect Continuous Tense is used for ongoing actions that started in the past and continue to the present.

Present Perfect Continuous Tense කියන්නේ අතීතයේ ආරම්භ වූ ක්‍රියා වර්තමානය දක්වා පවතින අවස්ථා විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Present Perfect Continuous Tense என்பது கடந்த காலத்தில் தொடங்கி தற்போது வரை தொடரும் செயல்களை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • Singular Subject + has been + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object
  • Plural Subject + have been + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object

Examples

  • Singular: She has been working here for 5 years.
  • Plural: They have been waiting since morning.
  • Plural: We have been studying for the exam.

Past Tenses

Simple Past Tense

Simple Past Tense is used for completed actions in the past at a specific time.

Simple Past Tense කියන්නේ නිශ්චිත කාලයක අතීතයේ සිදු වූ ක්‍රියා විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Simple Past Tense என்பது குறிப்பிட்ட நேரத்தில் முடிந்த செயல்களை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • All Subjects + Verb (Past form) + Object

Examples

  • She went to school yesterday.
  • They played football last week.
  • I finished my homework.

Past Continuous Tense

Past Continuous Tense is used for actions in progress at a specific past time or interrupted actions.

Past Continuous Tense කියන්නේ නිශ්චිත අතීත කාලයක සිදුවෙමින් පැවති ක්‍රියා හෝ අඛණ්ඩව සිදුවූ ක්‍රියා විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Past Continuous Tense என்பது குறிப்பிட்ட காலத்தில் நடந்து கொண்டிருந்த செயல்கள் அல்லது தடைப்பட்ட செயல்களை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • Singular Subject + was + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object
  • Plural Subject + were + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object

Examples

  • Singular: She was reading at 8pm.
  • Plural: They were playing when I arrived.
  • Plural: We were studying all night.

Past Perfect Tense

Past Perfect Tense is used for actions completed before another past action.

Past Perfect Tense කියන්නේ අතීතයේ එක් ක්‍රියාවක් තවත් ක්‍රියාවකට පෙර සිදු වූ අවස්ථා විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Past Perfect Tense என்பது ஒரு செயல் மற்றொரு கடந்த கால செயலுக்கு முன் நடந்தது என்பதை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • All Subjects + had + Verb (Past participle) + Object

Examples

  • She had left before I arrived.
  • They had finished dinner by 8pm.
  • We had seen the movie before.

Past Perfect Continuous Tense

Past Perfect Continuous Tense is used for duration before something in the past.

Past Perfect Continuous Tense කියන්නේ අතීතයේ යම් සිදුවීමකට පෙර කාලයක් පුරා පැවති ක්‍රියා විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Past Perfect Continuous Tense என்பது கடந்த காலத்தில் ஒரு குறிப்பிட்ட நேரத்திற்கு முன் தொடர்ந்து நடந்து கொண்டிருந்த செயல்களை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • All Subjects + had been + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object

Examples

  • I had been working for 3 hours.
  • They had been waiting since morning.
  • She had been studying all day.

Future Tenses

Simple Future Tense

Simple Future Tense is used for actions that will happen in the future.

Simple Future Tense කියන්නේ අනාගතයේ සිදුවන ක්‍රියා විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Simple Future Tense என்பது எதிர்காலத்தில் நடக்க இருக்கும் செயல்களை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • All Subjects + will + Verb (Base form) + Object

Examples

  • I will call you tomorrow.
  • They will arrive at 8pm.
  • She will finish her project.

Future Continuous Tense

Future Continuous Tense is used for actions in progress at a specific future time.

Future Continuous Tense කියන්නේ නිශ්චිත අනාගත කාලයක සිදුවෙමින් පවතින ක්‍රියා විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Future Continuous Tense என்பது எதிர்காலத்தில் ஒரு குறிப்பிட்ட நேரத்தில் நடந்து கொண்டிருக்கும் செயல்களை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • All Subjects + will be + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object

Examples

  • I will be flying to London this time tomorrow.
  • They will be having dinner at 8pm.
  • She will be working all day.

Future Perfect Tense

Future Perfect Tense is used for actions completed before a specific future time.

Future Perfect Tense කියන්නේ නිශ්චිත අනාගත කාලයකට පෙර අවසන් වන ක්‍රියා විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Future Perfect Tense என்பது எதிர்காலத்தில் ஒரு குறிப்பிட்ட நேரத்திற்கு முன் முடிந்திருக்கும் செயல்களை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • All Subjects + will have + Verb (Past participle) + Object

Examples

  • By 2025, I will have graduated.
  • They will have finished by 5pm.
  • She will have completed the project.

Future Perfect Continuous Tense

Future Perfect Continuous Tense is used for duration before something in the future.

Future Perfect Continuous Tense කියන්නේ අනාගතයේ යම් සිදුවීමකට පෙර කාලයක් පුරා පැවති ක්‍රියා විස්තර කිරීමට භාවිතා කරයි.

Future Perfect Continuous Tense என்பது எதிர்காலத்தில் ஒரு குறிப்பிட்ட நேரத்திற்கு முன் தொடர்ந்து நடந்து கொண்டிருக்கும் செயல்களை வெளிப்படுத்த பயன்படுகிறது.

Structure

  • All Subjects + will have been + Verb (Base form) + ing + Object

Examples

  • By 2025, I will have been working here for 5 years.
  • They will have been traveling for 3 months.
  • She will have been studying all night.
